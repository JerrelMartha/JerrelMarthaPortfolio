Spell-Based Player Controller (Just Begun)

A highly responsive character controller enabling dynamic projectile, beam, and area-of-effect spells. Custom casting physics, velocity formulas, and dynamic variables adjustable in real-time. This project is currently in its early stages of development.

C# Unity Gameplay Physics WIP
01. Overview

Character Casting Engine

In action RPGs and magic-based games, the player controller must feel incredibly fluid and allow complex physics integrations for spell casting. This project showcases a custom C# Unity player controller that handles complex spell physics, casting states, and modular spell definitions.

The core architecture uses a decoupling methodology: the Player Controller handles locomotion, state machines, and input processing, while a modular Spell Casting Component manages casting states, cooldowns, and instantiates customizable physical spells.

Modular Magic

Decoupled ScriptableObject spell templates that store payload, behaviors, and visual effects

Custom Physics

Spells support custom gravity factors, drag, bounce physics, and orbital curves

02. Gameplay Mechanics

Dynamic Variable Cast System

Built for a fast-paced movement system, this controller merges fluid locomotion with a tactical, high-risk casting loop. Instead of selecting static spells, you actively shape casting physics in real-time, utilizing your limited mana pool to prioritize the stats your immediate situation demands.

Hold Left-Click

Spell Velocity

Accelerates the projectile flight speed, flattening gravity-based arcs and hitting distant targets faster.

Hold Right-Click

Spell Size

Scales up the physical volume and collision radius, ensuring impact coverage over a wider visual area.

Hold Shift

Spell Intensity

Amplifies the core payload force or damage yield, releasing a much more explosive energy release upon impact.

Mana Constraints & Decision Making

Every millisecond spent holding down these inputs to tune variables drains your maximum mana pool. You are forced to choose what variable to tweak the most and optimize the casting outcome before your resource reserves completely deplete.
